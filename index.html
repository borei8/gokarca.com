Signature Flavours Lab

R&D is where your next best-seller is born. Below are a few of my in-house bases—click any tile to see the recipe, shelf-life and perfect serve.

Lime-Basil Shrub

Profile

Bright citrus bite balanced by herbaceous freshness; zero-alcohol base.

Recipe
  • Fresh lime juice — 1 kg
  • Caster sugar — 900 g
  • Basil leaves — 150 g
  • Rice vinegar — 250 ml
  • Cold maceration 12 h ➔ fine strain ➔ bottle
Uses & Pairing

• Adds a zesty kick to spritzes
• Pairs with blanco tequila, dry gin, tonic highballs
• Shelf-life 21 days refrigerated
